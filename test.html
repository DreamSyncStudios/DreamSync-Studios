Gmail Account Tip:

If you have multiple Gmail accounts logged in and the 'Gmail' button uses the wrong one, your browser is choosing the default session. To fix this, you may need to **open a blank Gmail tab first** and manually switch to the correct account, then try the button again, or use an **Incognito/Private window** where you are only logged into the one account you want to use.
